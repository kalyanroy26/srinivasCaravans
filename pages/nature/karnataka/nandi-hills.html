Nandi Hills

Nandi Hills 4.6

Experience the Sunrise at Nandi Hills

Perched at 1,478 meters above sea level, Nandi Hills offers panoramic views of rolling mist-clad valleys and a spectacular sunrise that draws travelers and photographers alike.

Best Time to Visit

October to February brings cool, clear mornings—perfect for catching the sunrise. Weekdays are less crowded than weekends, so plan accordingly.

Key Highlights

• Sunrise Viewpoint: Arrive before dawn to see the horizon blaze in gold and pink.
• Tipu Sultan’s Summer Residence: Historic fort ruins with scenic overlooks.
• Bhoga Nandeeshwara Temple: Ancient architecture nestled at the hill’s base.
• Cycling & Trekking Trails: Gentle slopes ideal for outdoor enthusiasts.
• Autumn & Monsoon Blooms: Lush greenery and seasonal wildflowers.

How Srinivasa Caravans Takes You There

Start early from Bengaluru in our air-conditioned caravans with professional drivers. We handle permits, picnic setups, and return transfers—so you can relax and enjoy the vista.

Visitor Tips

Carry warm clothing—it gets chilly at the summit. Bring binoculars for birdwatching and a flashlight for the pre-dawn hike. Snacks and bottled water are essential.

Highlights

  • Pre-dawn pickup and drop-off service.
  • Guided walk through historic fort ruins.
  • Optional cycling routes around the hillock.
  • Breakfast picnic arranged at the summit.
  • Photo stops at Amrita Sarovar Lake and view decks.
